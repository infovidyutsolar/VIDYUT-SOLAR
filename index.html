How Does Solar Energy Work?

A solar power system works in three simple steps:

  • Solar Panels capture sunlight and convert it into DC electricity.
  • Solar Inverter converts DC electricity into usable AC power.
  • Electricity Distribution Panel supplies power to your home or business.

Any extra electricity can be exported to the grid (in on-grid systems) or stored in batteries (in off-grid or hybrid systems).
